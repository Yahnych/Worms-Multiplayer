Worms

How to play and the controls

The text on the top left corner under the map is who is on turn. If someone didn't named themselves it will show their ids. When it is your turn you have to kill the others. The inventory for the weapons will show if you click "I". The bazooka takes 80 dmg and remove the terrain, the ak takes a lot of damage, the pistol takes little damage.

Keybinds:
ESC - settings menu
I - inventory
A and D - movement
Mouse on click - shoot
